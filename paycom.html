Specs

I work on a variety of Applicant Tracking and Human Capital Management products at Paycom, the online payroll and human resource technology provider. I have experience being a dedicated designer on agile teams as well as conducting user research through interviews, card sorts, and surveys. A majority of my work is behind NDA, but here's a brief look at some of the projects I have been involved with:

Applicant Tracking Analytics Dashboard

Visualizations for monitoring the number of views, applications, offers, and hires for a postion. As well as a visualization for monitoring which career sites and job boards are getting the most applicants.

Applicant Tracking Candidate Profile

An updated Candidate Profile to help streamline a recruiters view of a candidate.

Organizational Chart

Lastly, I helped with early designs for an updated Organizational chart that empowers HR Managers to easily take actions on their employees.
